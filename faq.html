Frequently asked questions

Where should I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Please post an issue here.

Is the extension open source?

Yes, you can browse the repository here.

Where is my data stored?

Your bookmark and preference data is stored in your Firefox profile folder. Only if you opt-in to sync your data across devices will your data be passed through the Firefox Sync servers.

What cryptographic library is used to encrypt my data?

The extension uses the Web Crypto API.

Where can I donate to show my appreciation?

Thanks, but I don't accept donations. If you want to show your support you can instead make a contribution to your preferred medical research or animal protection organization.
